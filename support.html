Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't have access to it, and it never leaves your phone.

What happens if I delete the app?

If you delete Singil, all your data will be permanently removed. We recommend settling your balances before uninstalling.

Can I sync data between devices?

Currently, Singil stores data locally on one device. Multi-device sync may be added in a future update.

How do I add a person?

Go to the People tab and tap "+ Add Person". Enter their name and optionally add a description.

How do I split a bill?

From the Home screen, tap "Split Bill". Add the people involved, enter the total, and choose equal split or split by item.

How do I record a payment?

Tap on a person's name to view their details, then tap "Record Payment" to mark partial or full settlement.
